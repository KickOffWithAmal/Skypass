Documents Required

To apply for a New Zealand visa, you generally need:

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Completed visa application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of financial means (bank statements, etc.)
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation details
  • Travel insurance

Processing Time

Our standard timeline includes a maximum of 5 working days for documentation preparation, followed by an embassy processing period of approximately 15 to 25 working days. Note that times may vary based on individual circumstances.

Visa Fee

Visa fees vary based on the specific type of visa (e.g., tourist vs. business) and current embassy regulations. Please contact us for the most up-to-date fee structure for New Zealand.

Eligibility

Eligibility criteria depend on the purpose of your visit. Generally, applicants must demonstrate strong ties to their home country, sufficient funds for the trip, and a clear intention to return after the visit.
