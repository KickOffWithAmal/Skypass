Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our visa services.

What is the typical processing time?
We offer a fast-track visa processing. Our standard timeline includes a maximum of 5 working days for documentation, handled by our highly skilled documentation team, followed by an embassy processing period of 15 to 25 working days. Processing times may vary depending on the respective embassy and individual case requirements.
Do I need to visit your office in person?
While we love meeting our clients at our office, we can handle most of the process digitally via email and WhatsApp.
What is your success rate?
We maintain a success rate of over 95%. Our experts thoroughly screen every profile before submission to ensure the highest chances of approval.
